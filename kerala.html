Arrive Cochin
Arrive Cochin Station / Airport and reach our hotel directly (Hotel Check/in:- 12:00 noon)& rest the of the day is free for rest / shopping. Night halt at Cochin.
Cochin - Munnar
After breakfast visit Dutch Palace, St. Francis Church and Chinese Fishing Nets. After lunch proceed to Munnar (5-6 hrs. journeys), en route visit the famous waterfalls. Night halt at Munnar.
Munnar
After breakfast visit Metupatty Dam for Speed boating & Coconut Water (at our cost) and Rose Garden. After lunch visit Hydel Park. Night halt at Munnar.
Munnar
After breakfast visit Rajmala view point the highest point of Kerala. After lunch the day is free to explore the natural beauty of Munnar. Night halt at Munnar.
Note: Munnar - Eravikulam National Park Closed for 2 Months in a year due to the calving season, in this period we Visit to Tea Museum.
Munnar - Thekkady
After breakfast proceed to Thekkady (5 hrs. journey), en route visit the Tea Gardens of Munnar. Arrive Thekkady and check-in at hotel. In the evening take a guided Spice Plantation tour and enjoy Kerala Traditional Kathakali show (at our Cost).Night halt at Thekkady.
Thekkady - Kumarakom
After breakfast enjoy boating at Periyar Lake OR Jeep Safari (at your own cost) / shopping etc. After lunch proceed to Kumarakom (6 hrs. journey). Night halt at Kumarakom.
Kumarakom
After breakfast morning free to enjoy resort facilities. After lunch enjoy Kerala's famous back waters ride for 3 hrs (at our Cost).. Night halt at Kumarakom.
Kumarakom - Kanyakumari
After breakfast proceed to Kanyakumari (10 hrs. journey). In the evening sightseeing tour of Kanyakumari which includes Gandhi Mandap, Triveni Sangam Ghat and Sunset Point. Night halt at Kanyakumari.
Kanyakumari - Poovar Island
Morning view the Sunrise from the hotel Terrace. After breakfast visit to Vivekananda Rock Memorial (Special Pass by DDG).After lunch proceed to Poovar (3 hrs. journey). Evening is free at leisure. Night halt at Poovar Island.
Poovar Island - Trivandrum - Poovar Island
After breakfast visit to Padmanabh Swamy Temple & Kovalam Beach. After lunch enjoy at beach. Night halt at Poovar Island.
Train Journey
After breakfast transfer to Trivandrum railway station / Airport to board your train / Flight for Mumbai ( flight to be booked after 11.00 a.m ) with memorable experience of the tour conducted by Heena Tours & Travels. Passengers departing early / late by train / Flight has to transfer station / airport at their own expense.
This tour starts with Lunch on Day 1 and ends with Breakfast on Day 11.

Travel Tips for KERALA :
  • Carry your Regular Basic medicines & Basic First Aid Kit with you
  • Valid Photo Id (Aadhar Card/Driving License/Voter Id/Passport).
  • In Kerala Rain Showers are expected anytime throughout the year so carry Umbrella / Rain coat & also cover your travelling bag with plastic from inside so that your clothes don’t get wet.
  • Carry Sun Glasses, Sun’s screen, Moisturizer, Cap, Sport Shoes
  • Carry 1 Jacket or Sweater
  • There is a special dress code for Padmanabh Swamy Temple in Trivandrum. For Men Dhoti with or without Angavastra,For Womens are Sarees. Young girls below 12 years may wear gowns. You can buy from outside the temple or can carry your own.
